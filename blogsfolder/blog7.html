Analyzing Website Designs: Johannesburg Zoo and Parktown Boys

The Johannesburg Zoo
https://www.jhbcityparksandzoo.com/services-facilities/zoo/about

Why: The Johannesburg Zoo is a well-known public space, and I wanted to study how they present information. Interestingly, they use a lot of design practices that I plan to avoid. Reflecting on this helps clarify my own design approach.

Pros:

  • Extensive presentation of information.
  • High contrast: black font on a white background.
  • Simple text styling enhances readability.
  • Uniform layout across all pages.

Cons:

  • The website is overwhelming, linking to too many different pages.
  • Too many navigation bars clutter the user experience.
  • The pages lack unique identity and feel generic.

Screenshot: Screenshot 2025-03-29 215004.png

Parktown Boys
https://parktownboys.com/

Why: All-boys high schools often have strong identities and loyal communities. Their branding is critical, so I wanted to see how they communicate it through design.

Pros:

  • They use colour palettes I’m interested in — bright fonts on dark backgrounds.
  • UI elements like sticky nav bars, reactive buttons, footers, and rounded icons enhance usability and match my design goals.

Cons:

  • Many of their internal links lead to separate websites, making navigation confusing and inconsistent.
