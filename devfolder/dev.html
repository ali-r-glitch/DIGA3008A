Egyptian Game

The Egyptian game is a narrative puzzle game. The player is transported into a book where they disrupt the reincarnation of the god Osiris. The player must traverse egypt to recollect all the body parts to restart the reincarnation of the god and restore history.

Helicopter Game

This is a character controller focused traversal game. The player must collect water and disperse it over burning buildings. If three buildings burn down, the player loses.

Active Ragdoll

This is a prototype inspired by Octodad. It is an active ragdoll that is difficult to traverse the world woth. The players goal is to complete simple tasks with the character
