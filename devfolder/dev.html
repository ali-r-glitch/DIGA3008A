    • Egyptian game

    The Egyptian game is a narrative puzzle game where the player is a historian who is trapped in a book and interrupts the resurrections of the god Osiris. The player must journey to recover the body parts and continue the resurrections to escape the book and restore History.

    Helicopter game

    The helicopter game is a simple game that is played by pressing the space bar to make the helicopter go up and releasing the space bar to make the helicopter go down. The objective of the game is to put out buildings that are on fire. The game ends of 3 buiildings burn down.
